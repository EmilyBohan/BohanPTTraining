About Emily Bohan, Founder of Bohan PT & Training

Learn about my journey in health and wellness, and what I can do for you.

With a combined 10 years of experience as a physical therapist, personal trainer, nutrition coach, and athlete, my goal is to help each and every client feel like the most optimal version of themselves.

Why Me?

My professional journey into health and wellness began when I became a personal trainer at the University of Florida in 2015. I had the privilege of training and mentorship with highly qualified specialists, and worked with individuals from first time gym-goers to semi-professional athletes. I attained my 200 hour Yoga Alliance Teacher Certification in 2015, giving me a well-rounded appreciation for the mental and physical benefits of moving our bodies. In 2021 I obtained my Doctorate in Physical Therapy, and have specialized in orthopedics, sports, and pelvic health. I have worked with many individuals with hypermobility, including those diagnosed with hEDS.

My personal wellness journey began in 2013, when I became very involved in sports, yoga, weightlifting, and educating myself about the importance of nutrition for both our physical and emotional body. Despite challenges presented to me from hypermobile joints, I went on to compete in multiple 5k races, powerlifting, and obstacle course racing. My athletic endeavors also showed me the importance of pelvic health in both personal life and sports endeavors. Today, I love balancing all athletic endeavors, as I enjoy a hike in the mountains with my dog Skye as well as weightlifting. I also make time to snowboard in the beautiful Colorado mountains, and horseback riding.
