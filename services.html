Expectations at First Visit

We will discuss your symptoms, evaluate all muscle groups that can be contributing to your symptom presentation such as your hips, back and abdominals, and discuss the influence of pelvic floor on your symptom presentation.

Pelvic Floor treatment occasionally involves an internal examination of the muscles, on your own in the comfort of your own home. If you are not comfortable with this exam, there are plenty of non-direct treatment options. However, function of pelvic floor is best assessed directly, and so at your first visit this may be necessary to understanding the full picture to your symptoms.
